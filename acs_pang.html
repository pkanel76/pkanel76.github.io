Keyboard: arrows or A/D to move, space to fire (hold for the Vulcan), P to pause. Touch: on-screen buttons, or tap the game area to fire. Every hit scores 100 points. From level 3, every 5th hit drops an ACS locker (+300 points, random weapon for 15 s). Parcels drop at random and give +200 points and an extra life (up to 3 extra lives per game). Presents disappear 1 second after landing, so move fast. Top 5 scores are saved in this browser.
