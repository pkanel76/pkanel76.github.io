Integrating Urban Factors as Predictors of Last-Mile Demand Patterns: A Spatial Analysis in Thessaloniki

D. Touloumidis, M. Madas, P. Kanellopoulos, G. Ayfantopoulou · Urban Science 9(8), 293 · 2025

Combines one year of courier delivery data with open spatial layers — land use, census population, mobile-signal activity and income — to model where last-mile demand concentrates in a city, supporting decisions like locker allocation, micro-depots and curb-space management.
